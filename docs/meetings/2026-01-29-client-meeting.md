# Client Meeting Notes – January 29, 2026

## Client
Mario Moubarak meeting with Capstone students: Michael Haddad, Henry Saber & Mathew Aoun

## What Was Shown

- Current application structure and navigation
- Basic Dashboard overview with portfolio value, risk score, and diversification
- Early concepts for risk exploration and analysis

## What the Client Liked

- Clear structure and separation of pages (Dashboard, Assets, Portfolio, Risk)
- The idea of focusing on *risk understanding* rather than just performance numbers
- The idea of clicking into risks to learn more about what is driving them

## Client Feedback / Requests

- Dashboard feels useful but should stay focused on the most important information
  - Keep overall portfolio value, risk score, and key risks
  - Avoid overcrowding the dashboard with too many sections
- Wants the ability to click into “Top Risks” to see more detailed explanations
- Likes the idea of separating **Assets** (individual holdings) & **Portfolio** (accounts like TFSA, RRSP, etc)
- Requested that the interface feel more polished and visually engaging
  - Add colors and clearer visual grouping
  - Make the app feel more modern and less “plain”
- Did NOT like idea of adding Giveaways to Alerts section
